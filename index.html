Jinjie Ni

Hi there! I'm Jinjie. My friends call me Oliver as well. I'm currently a Ph.D. candidate under the supervision of Prof. Erik Cambria at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore. Being a big fan of road trips, I also love working out at the gym, playing basketball, and watch various games and TV shows.

I am interested in researching Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing related problems.
