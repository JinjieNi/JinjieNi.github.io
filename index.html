Jinjie Ni

a.k.a. Oliver

I'm currently a Research Fellow at National University of Singapore with Prof. Yang You. I finished my Ph.D. at Nanyang Technological University (2020-2023), School of Computer Science and Engineering. I was a research intern at DAMO Alibaba SG, Harvard University, Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Continental. When I'm not deeply engrossed in research, you can find me indulging in powerlifting, capturing moments through photography, playing ball games, or strategizing over a game of poker.

I study Large Language Models and Artificial General Intelligence.

Previously, I was interested in improving the efficiency of general tasks and made some explorations in model compression and efficient multi-modal alignment. I also spent much time improving conversational agents and unifying the dialogue system domain.

I will be on the job market shortly, looking out for new career opportunities as a research scientist working on Large Language Models or Artificial General Intelligence. If you know some companies or individuals who may be looking for someone with my expertise and experience, please do not hesitate to reach out to me directly.
