JINJIE NI

Hi there! I'm Jinjie. I'm currently a final year Ph.D. candidate under the supervision of Prof. Erik Cambria at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore. Before the graduation of my Ph.D., I took several research internships at institutions such as DAMO Alibaba SG, Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Harvard University. Being a big fan of road trips, I also love working out at the gym, playing basketball, and watching various games and TV shows.

My research interest falls under the Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing areas. During my Ph.D., I have gained expertise in exploring efficient/explainable methods for general NLP tasks, including model compression, efficient & explainable knowledge distillation, and neural-symbolic learning. I’m enthusiastic to improve the deployability of large models and the efficacy of fine-tuning schemes to accelerate the process towards AGI and would love to bring my skills and knowledge to a new team.

I'm on the lookout for new career opportunities as an NLP/AI research scientist or postdoc. If you know some companies or individuals who may be looking for someone with my expertise and experience, please do not hesitate to reach out to me directly. Many thanks.
