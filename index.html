Jinjie Ni

Hi there! I'm Jinjie. My friends call me Oliver as well. I'm currently a final year Ph.D. candidate under the supervision of Prof. Erik Cambria at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore. Being a big fan of road trips, I also love working out at the gym, playing basketball, and watching various games and TV shows. Before the graduation of my Ph.D., I took several research internships at institutions such as DAMO Alibaba SG, Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Harvard University.

My research interest falls under the Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing areas. I have been interested in exploring more efficient/explainable methods for NLP tasks, including efficient archs for NLP, efficient & explainable knowledge distillation for NLP, and knowledge-grounded NLP. Being keen to follow the latest topics in the community, I'm also open to new possible research focuses.

I'm interested in joining well-matched research teams of universities/companies. Do not hesitate to contact me for more knowledge!
